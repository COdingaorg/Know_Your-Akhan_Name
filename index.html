Hello and Welcome. Lets have some fun shall we. This websites shows you the day of the week you were born and you Akan name. Akan name are cultural names given to children depending on your gender and day of the week you were born. You will be required to enter your birthday date in the correct format and select your Gender. We will then show you the day of the week and your Akan name :). Awesome right?!
